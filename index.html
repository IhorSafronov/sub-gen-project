What is Sonos?

Sonos Sub features:
  • 2 Class D digital amplifiers
  • 2 reciprocating bass drivers for deeper, cleaner, richer sound without noise or vibration
  • Physical power button on the side of the unitLED status indicator
  • Two speaker ports
  • Frequency range: from 25 Hz
  • Automatic sound adjustment ensures a perfect balance between the subwoofer and Sonos or other components to optimise sound quality.
  • Power supply: 100-240V, 50-60Hz AC
  • Wi-Fi (802.11b/g; 2.4 GHz)
  • 1 x 10/100 Mbps Ethernet port
  • Colour: glossy black, glossy white
  • Dimensions: 389 x 402 x 158 mm
  • Weight: 16 kg

Supplied with: Sonos Sub, power cord, quick start guide, manufacturer's warranty.

Voice control*. Use the built-in Sonos, Google, Alexa (*on compatible models) voice assistant to control your music and devices hands-free.
